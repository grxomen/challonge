Economy Commands

.daily

Claim your daily petals and essence. Requires 30 words in SFW/NSFW today. Streaks give bonus rewards, VIP gets 2x!

.balance .bal

Check your garden wallet. Shows all three currencies, streak, tier, and today's activity progress.

.gforge

Forge a radiant from 300 petals + 100 essence. The only way to obtain this prestige currency!

.gleaderboard .glb

View top earners in the garden. See who's accumulated the most currency!

.dailystats .dstats

Detailed statistics on your daily claims, total earned, and tier progression.

.streaks

Leaderboard of longest daily streaks. Who has the most consecutive days?

.gtasks

View available tasks. Complete them to earn petals and essence rewards.

.gcomplete <task_id>

Complete a task and claim your rewards. Some tasks are repeatable!

.gshop [category]

Browse the garden shop. Filter by category to see specific items with prices and stock.

.gbuy <item_id>

Purchase an item from the shop. Interactive confirmation with balance verification.

.ggift @user <amount> <currency>

Gift petals or essence to friends. Radiant cannot be gifted!
