💫 Economy Commands

.gdaily / .gd Claim your daily petals and essence (VIP 2x bonus)
.gbalance / .gbal Check your wallet — all three currencies at a glance
.gforge Forge 1 radiant from 100 petals + 50 essence
.gleaderboard / .glb Top earners leaderboard — tier rankings and totals
.gstats Your detailed economy statistics and history
.gtasks View available tasks and rewards
.gcomplete <task> Complete a task to earn currency
.gshop [category] Browse the garden shop — items, commissions, perks
.gbuy <item_id> Purchase an item from the shop
.ginventory View your purchase history and claimed items
